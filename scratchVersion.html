Meet the CEO



Alethea Nichols-Taylor

Professional Bio



Alethea Nichols-Taylor is an expert on authentic leadership and meaningful work. She has transformed the lives of thousands of professionals from around the world in her professional events, seminars, workshops and coaching circles. With over 20 years of transformational coaching and leadership development experience, Alethea is a rare blend of social entrepreneur, visionary, teacher, and coach.

As an event expert, she is known for blending transformational inner work with practical results, and for creating highly supportive yet challenging productions where individuals expand their authentic leadership and realize their full potential power and impact. She has worked with senior leaders in private companies and start-ups, as well as organizations in the education and non-profit sectors.

In addition to her professional event management skills, Alethea teaches appreciative and gratitude courses that unleash a great potential in her clients. She is the President of Seasons by Design, LLC . Her business understands that people, business and organizations go through different seasons of change and they assist in navigating through the turbulence of them all.

Having lived and learned through the ups and downs of starting a mission-driven company, Alethea is passionate about supporting leaders who want to contribute their talents toward a calling that fully inspires them. As a wife and mother of two, she has a deep commitment to creating new models and pathways for making a leadership impact while raising a family, and to building a community and culture where men and women’s leadership thrives at unprecedented levels.
